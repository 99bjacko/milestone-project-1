DISCOGRAPHY

SHOWBIZ

The debut studio album was released in 1999 to mixed reviews from critics. It features 12 tracks, including the singles "Uno", "Cave", "Muscle Museum", "Sunburn" and "Unintended". Some of the songs on this record date back as far as 1996, when Muse were still an unsigned band.

TRACK LIST

1. Sunburn 2. Muscle Museum 3. Fillip 4. Falling Down 5. Cave 6. Showbiz 7. Unintended 8. Uno 9. Sober 10. Escape 11. Overdue 12. Hate This & I'll Love You
Spiral Static (Bonus Track)

ORIGIN OF SYMMETRY

Origin of Symmetry, Muse's second studio album, was released in 2001 and peaked at number three on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 11 tracks, including the singles "Plug In Baby", "New Born", "Bliss" and "Hyper Music / Feeling Good". The album received a plethora of positive reviews from critics, praising the album's originality. It has been ranked among the best rock albums of the 2000s and is considered some of the band's finest work.

TRACK LIST

1. New Born 2. Bliss 3. Space Dementia 4. Hyper Music 5. Plug In Baby 6. Citizen Erased 7. Micro Cuts 8. Screenager 9. Darkshines 10. Feeling Good 11. Megalomania
Futurism (Bonus Track)

ABSOLUTION

Absolution, Muse's third studio album, was released in 2003 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 14 tracks, including the singles "Stockholm Syndrome", "Hysteria", "Sing for Absolution", "Apocalypse Please", "Butterflies and Hurricanes", and "Time is Running Out", the latter being the band's first top-ten single. The album is said to have a more polished and direct sound when compared with their previous work. Muse experimented with orchestra for tracks "Butterflies and Hurricanes" and "Blackout", something they would dive into on later albums.

TRACK LIST

1. Intro 2. Apocalypse Please 3. Time is Running Out 4. Sing for Absolution 5. Stockholm Syndrome 6. Falling Away With You 7. Interlude 8. Hysteria 9. Blackout 10. Butterflies and Hurricanes 11. The Small Print 12. Endlessly 13. Thoughts of a Dying Atheist 14. Ruled by Secrecy
Fury (Bonus Track)

BLACK HOLES AND REVELATIONS

Black Holes and Revelations, Muse's fourth studio album, was released in 2006 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 11 tracks, including the singles "Starlight", "Knights of Cydonia", "Invincible", "Map of the Problematique", and "Supermassive Black Hole", the latter is still Muse's highest charting single, peaking at number four in the UK Singles Charts. Some of the band's most popular songs are on this record with "Knights of Cydonia" remaining a fan favourite and is still consistently used to close out Muse's live shows.

TRACK LIST

1. Take a Bow 2. Starlight 3. Supermassive Black Hole 4. Map of the Problematique 5. Soldier's Poem 6. Invincible 7. Assassin 8. Exo-Politics 9. City of Delusion 10. Hoodoo 11. Knights of Cydonia
Glorious (Bonus Track)

THE 2ND LAW

The 2nd Law, Muse's sixth studio album, was released in 2012 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 13 tracks, including the singles "Madness", "Follow Me", "Supremacy", "Panic Station" and "Survival", the latter being the official 2012 London Olympics theme song. "Madness" remains one of the band's most streamed tracks.

TRACK LIST

1. Supremacy 2. Madness 3. Panic Station 4. Prelude 5. Survival 6. Follow Me 7. Animals 8. Explorers 9. Big Freeze 10. Save Me 11. Liquid State 12. The 2nd Law: Unsustainable 13. The 2nd Law: Isolated System

DRONES

Drones, Muse's seventh studio album, was released in 2015 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 12 tracks, including the singles "Dead Inside", "Mercy", "Revolt", "Aftermath" and "Reapers". The album was the band trying to reconnect with their rock roots after delving into much experimentation on the last two albums.

TRACK LIST

1. Dead Inside 2. [Drill Sergeant] 3. Psycho 4. Mercy 5. Reapers 6. The Handler 7. [JFK] 8. Defector 9. Revolt 10. Aftermath 11. The Globalist 12. Drones

SIMULATION THEORY

Simulation Theory, Muse's eighth studio album, was released in 2018 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album features 11 tracks, including the singles "Dig Down", "Thought Contagion", "Something Human", "The Dark Side", and "Pressure". The album was also released as two deluxe editions, featuring additional alternate versions of its tracks. Compared with darker, more dystopian themes of previous albums, Simulation Theory is centralised around science fiction and 1980s pop culture.

TRACK LIST

1. Algorithm 2. The Dark Side 3. Pressure 4. Propaganda 5. Break It to Me 6. Something Human 7. Thought Contagion 8. Get Up and Fight 9. Blockades 10. "Dig Down" 11. The Void

WILL OF THE PEOPLE

Will of the People, Muse's ninth and most recent studio album, was released in 2022 and reached number one onf the UK Albums Chart. The album features 10 tracks, including the singles "Compliance", "Will of the People", "You Make Me Feel Like It's Halloween", "Kill or Be Killed" and "Won't Stand Down", the latter was the first track to be released, after it was teased by Matt Bellamy on Instagram in the prior December. Will of the People was described by Matt as "a greatest hits album - of new songs."

TRACK LIST

1. Will of the People 2. Compliance 3. Liberation 4. Won't Stand Down 5. Ghosts (How Can I Move On) 6. You Make Me Feel Like It's Halloween 7. Kill or Be Killed 8. Verona 9. Euphoria 10. We Are F**king F**ked
