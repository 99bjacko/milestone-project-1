GIGS

The O2 Arena

September 14th, 2019

After recently getting back into the band's music with their most recent release at the time, Simulation Theory, this was my first ever concert. It was unlike anything I had ever experienced, with incredible stage design, everything looked and sounded incredible.

Eventim Apollo

9th May 2022

Almost 3 years after my first Muse concert, I wanted to make sure I could get to see them again as soon as possible. This gig was the second time the band played live since the start of the pandemic. This show remains my favourite to this day, the setlist was perfect and after listening to their music practically non stop since 2019, it was one of the best nights of my life.

SETLIST

1. Won't Stand Down 2. Interlude + Hysteria 3. Pressure 4. Bliss 5. Drill Sergeant + Psycho 6. Assassin (Grand Omega Bosses Edit) 7. The Gallery (Live Debut) 8. Compliance (Guitar Version) 9. Stockholm Syndrome 10. Prelude + Starlight 11. Plug In Baby 12. Citizen Erased 13. Supermassive Black Hole 14. Uprising 15. Space Dementia 16. Knights of Cydonia

Home Park

27th May 2023

Just over a year after the Apollo in London, Muse returned to Devon, the place where they grew up. While this show did not have the same incredible setlist that was featured in May 2022, the setlist was filled with new songs that I had wanted to hear live, plus both the support acts were superb, one of which, Royal Blood, have become one of my favourite artists alongside Muse. All of this on a beautiful, warm day to top it off.

SETLIST

1. Will of the People 2. Interlude + Hysteria 3. Drill Sergeant + Psycho 4. Bliss 5. Resistance 6. Won't Stand Down 7. Kill or Be Killed (Felsmann + Tiley Reinterpretation) 8. Compliance 9. Thought Contagion 10. Verona 11. Time is Running Out 12. Isolated System (shortened) 13. Undisclosed Desires 14. You Make Me Feel Like It's Halloween 15. Madness 16. We Are F**king F**ked 17. The Dark Side (Alternate Reality Version) (Instrumental) 18. Supermassive Black Hole 19. Plug In Baby 20. Behold, The Glove 21. Uprising 22. Prelude + Starlight 23. Simulation Theory Theme / JFK + Kill or Be Killed 24. Knights of Cydonia
